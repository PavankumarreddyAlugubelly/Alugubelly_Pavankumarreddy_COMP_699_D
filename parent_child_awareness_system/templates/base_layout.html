Welcome to Parent–Child Daily Awareness System

This platform helps parents understand daily routine patterns of their children in a responsible and privacy-friendly way. Instead of tracking personal content, the system focuses only on activity-based insights such as study time, sleep patterns, screen usage, and spending behavior.

By identifying routine consistency and unusual changes early, this system supports better communication and timely guidance between parents and children.
